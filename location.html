How to get
here

From Bangkok, head south along the coast. The drive is about 2 hours and 20 minutes — most of it is windows-down beach road.

Open in Google Maps Open in Apple Maps
By car
2h 20m from BangkokTake Phetkasem Road south. Free parking on site for 18 cars and a small motorbike lot at the rear.
By train
3h from Hua LamphongTake the train to Cha-am Station, then a 12-minute songthaew or taxi to the beach road.
By bus
2h 30m from Bangkok Southern Bus TerminalDirect minivans run hourly. Ask for the Cha-am beach stop.
Walking
2 min from the shoreWalk straight from the main beach pier, cross Narathat Road. We're the building with the wide blue awning.

Parking & access

We've kept things simple. Free, attended parking on site — and the beach is one short walk away.
