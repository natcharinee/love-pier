The fine print

01

Confirmation

You'll receive an email or LINE message within 2 hours confirming your reservation. If we miss you, we'll call.

02

Group bookings

For groups of 7 or more, please call us directly. We hold a private corner for parties up to 24.

03

Late arrivals

We hold your table for 15 minutes past the reserved time. After that, we may offer it to walk-ins.

04

Cancellations

Free cancellation up to 4 hours before. Same-day no-shows may affect future reservation priority.
